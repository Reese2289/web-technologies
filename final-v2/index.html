About Monster Hunter

Monster Hunter is a game series created by Capcom that allows the player to create a character called a "hunter". While playing as your created hunter you can hunt a variety of monsters and craft armor/weapons using materials gained from completing hunts. There are a total of fourteen weapons to pick from, each having their own unique playstyle.
