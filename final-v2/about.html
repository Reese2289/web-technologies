About Hunter

I first started playing Monster Hunter around 2018. I began Monster Hunter World with some friends and named my hunter "Shuzu". Shuzu is typically what I name myself when I play games, so this name was going to stick with my hunter for all Monster Hunter iterations. I played all of Monster Hunter World and only used the Longsword weapon. After I beat the base game I continued onto the expansion called "Iceborne". After I completed the DLC (Downloadable Content) I waited until Monster Hunter Rise was released. I played all of Monster Hunter risehunter and got very close to finishing the expansion "Sunbreak" for the game as well.
