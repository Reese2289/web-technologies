My Monster Hunter Experience

I have been playing monster hunter ever since I started playing Monster Hunter World(MHW). The game was hard to get into at first but when I met some friends who enjoyed the game I was able to commit to it. I named my character Shuzu and named my palice Sphinx. I will continue to use these names for the rest of my Monster Hunter experience.

Loadout

In Monster Hunter you are able to build a very specific loadout if you wanted to. This includes but not limited to:

  • Weapon(s)
  • Armor
  • Consumables
  • Talismans
  • Decorations

Favorite Weapons

There are a total of 14 distinct weapons in the Monster Hunter franchise:

  1. Greatsword
  2. Long Sword
  3. Hunting Horn
  4. Gunlance
  5. Lance
  6. Dual Blades
  7. Hammer
  8. Bow
  9. Light Bowgun
  10. Heavy Bowgun
  11. Insect Glaive
  12. Sword and Shield
  13. Switchaxe
  14. Charge Blade

I tried to use all of the weapons but I ended up sticking with these weapons:

Long Sword
A swift and agile sword that allows for fast movements as well as high damage.
Insect Glaive
A swift and agile glaive that comes with two components, the glaive and the kinsect. Varies between arial and ground combos.
Dual Blades
Two blades with a swift playstyle that uses dodges and fast attacks.
